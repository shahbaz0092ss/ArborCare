Social Activties

At home

  • Playing cards or board-games
  • Reading or listening to audio books
  • Sitting and relaxing in the garden, while listening to music
  • Staying in touch with family and friends via social media (Facebook, Skype, FaceTime, etc.)

Physical Activities

Outside the house

  • Going to the restaurant for lunch or dinner
  • Going shopping at the mall or visiting the hair salon
  • Going to the bank or any other store of choice
  • Getting exercise with a pleasant stroll around the block or to a park
  • Going to the gym or a fitness class
  • Going to a community center for classes, workshops, or to meet friends
